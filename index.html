Our Services

Agile Coaching, Scrum Master Services, Individual Coaching, Training and Faciliation

We provide Agile Coaching, Scrum Master Services and individual coaching. We also supply onsite training, organisation and facilitation of bespoke events and give meetup and conference talks to the community. We have experience and understanding of LeSS, SAFe and other scaling frameworks and have worked with teams to implement scaling as appropriate. Available workshops and facilitated events include psychology for agile teams, Story Mapping, Example Mapping, Wardley Mapping, Agile, Scrum and Kanban training, multi team retrospectives and GV Design Sprints.
